Project Overview

What is Pitch AI?

Pitch AI is a sophisticated web application that allows users to play the classic card game Pitch against AI opponents powered by neural networks trained with reinforcement learning. The AI demonstrates advanced strategic thinking in both bidding and playing phases of the game.

Key Features

  • Complete 4-player Pitch game implementation
  • Three AI opponents using trained neural networks
  • Interactive UI with card visualization
  • Real-time gameplay with visual feedback
  • Auto-play mode for AI vs AI games
  • Responsive design for all devices

Technical Implementation

Neural Network Architecture

The AI system uses two specialized neural networks:

  • Bidding Model: 56 inputs â†’ 128 hidden neurons â†’ 4 outputs (Pass, Bid 2, Bid 3, Bid 4)
  • Playing Model: 184 inputs â†’ 256 hidden neurons â†’ 52 outputs (one for each card)

State Representation

The AI processes complex game states including:

  • Current hand (52-card encoding)
  • Trump suit information
  • Current trick state
  • Game history and bidding results
  • Player position and turn information

Decision Making

The AI employs policy gradient methods with actor-critic architecture, legal move masking for valid actions, and stochastic sampling for exploration. This creates a challenging opponent that adapts its strategy based on game context.

Game Rules

How to Play Pitch

Pitch is a trick-taking card game where 4 players compete in teams of 2:

  • Each player receives 6 cards
  • Players bid on how many points they can win (Pass, 2, 3, or 4)
  • Trump suit is determined by the first card played
  • Points are awarded for winning tricks and special cards
  • Teams must work together to achieve their bid

Controls

  • New Game: Start a new game
  • Auto Play: Watch AI vs AI games
  • Reset: Reset scores and return to waiting state
  • Bidding: Click bid buttons during bidding phase
  • Playing: Click cards in your hand to play them

Development Process

Training & Development

The AI models were developed using reinforcement learning techniques:

  • Models trained on thousands of simulated games
  • Policy gradient optimization for strategic decision making
  • Extensive testing and validation against rule-based opponents
  • Web deployment using TensorFlow.js for browser compatibility

Future Enhancements

Planned improvements include:

  • Online multiplayer support
  • Tournament mode for AI competition
  • Game replay and analysis tools
  • Additional game variants and rules
